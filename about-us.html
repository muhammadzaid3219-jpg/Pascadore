About Us

Pescadores Suites, one of the scenic destinations in the Philippines located at the south part of Cebu. This state of the art Santorini-inspired beachfront hotel, with 22 rooms, 5 villa and 3 suites, Our hotel offers contemporary style with a fun and fresh appeal for both leisure and relaxation. Offering an array of choices from standard, deluxe, suite rooms & villas, spa wellness, activities, and in-room entertainment. Positioned at the strip of neighboring hotels, restaurants and cafes making our property the most ideal choice of having a wonderful Moalboal experience.

In the heart of Basdiot Moalboal, the hotel is your perfect hideaway from which you can explore nearby waterfalls, dive sites, restaurants and the famous Pescador island which can be seen across the pool area. Diving enthusiasts are in for a breath-taking view of the underwater world as some of the country’s best diving sites are found outside the hotel. Turtles, and sardines are abundantly available to your viewing. If you are lucky, dolphins and threshers are also seen by chance.

Back in the hotel, there’s absolutely no room for boredom with limitless opportunities in both indoor and outdoor adventures for kids and adults alike. From swimming, snorkeling, spa wellness and game room area; rain or shine, you will be able to find something, no matter what interests you.
